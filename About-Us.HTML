About Us

Grant travel and tourism was estabilished in 2005 with a prime motive to handle domestic and inbound tourists. Grant travel and tourism was specifically started as tour operators and a travel agency. With leisure travel as as core business, the company is also involved into corporate travel solutions. Grant travel and tourism has a special fleet of cars, MUVs, luxury coaches(AC and non AC) and VOLVO buses. Grant travel and tourism has acces to huge infrastructure as well as, to more fine service development. Moreover, it still offers the personalized service that has always been its strength and ensures that the quality of service is maintained every time.
